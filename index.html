BESPOKE AUDIO DESIGN

It's Alive is a bespoke audio system design and installation service with its roots in technical production and events. We are a company with many decades of specialist, sound technology and AV experience among us.

​We've designed and installed audio systems for some of the most exciting and innovative venues around, including all 10 MeatLiquor restaurants and bars, Queens Ice Rink and Bowling and Nine Lives specialist cocktail venue.

​We design and deliver audio systems for venues all over the UK and the rest of the world, including Dubai, Vietnam, Thailand, Australia and Europe. London-based, national coverage, international consultancy.

WE LIVE AND BREATHE AUDIO

We work closely with a venues' shop-fitters, creating bespoke sound systems integrated into different audio zones within the building's construction and interior design.

Our systems are designed specifically around your venue needs.
The result is a finely-tuned system that fits the personality of each venue.​

A Highly customisable audio environment for instance:
Remote control of sound for different audio zones of the venue via mobile phones, tablets and wall panels.
Varied sound frequency settings and volume control to suit different audio zones within one space, or in different rooms and at different times of the day.

​Our systems are fully customisable and can be tailored to your exact design requirements.
